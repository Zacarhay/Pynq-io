## Development Boards

PYNQ supports Zynq based boards (Zynq, Zynq Ultrascale+, Zynq RFSoC), <a href="https://www.xilinx.com/products/som/kria.html">Kria SOMs</a>, Xilinx Alveo accelerator boards and AWS-F1 instances.

See the PYNQ Alveo Getting Started guide for details on installing PYNQ for use with Alveo and AWS-F1.
